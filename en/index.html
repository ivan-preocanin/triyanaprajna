Triyāna Prajñā School of Dharma

    Triyāna Prajñā School of Dharma is intended for those who are inspired to seek the meaning of Buddha's teachings and to integrate it into their experience.

    The program of study provides a foundational view of the great variety of Buddha's teachings, how they fit together, elucidate one another, and form a holistic system of instructions. The focal point of learning is to put understanding into practical use and integrate it into daily life.

    The three year long program will start in the spring of 2019.
